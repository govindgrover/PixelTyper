PixelTyper

Put precise text on images, fast.

PixelTyper is a simple desktop tool for creators, teams, and anyone who needs consistent text overlays on images. Add one-off labels, build reusable templates, and batch-apply them with confidence.

Designers & creators
Generate social graphics, name cards, and overlays without wrestling with heavy tools.
Ops & back-office
Stamp dates, names, or IDs on images for documentation or compliance.
Teams & agencies
Use templates for consistent placement across many assets.

Open-source, privacy-friendly, and built for speed.
